Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

The Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze Amazon sales performance, customer behavior, fulfillment trends, and product insights. The dashboard provides stakeholders with a comprehensive view of sales metrics, category performance, order distribution, and geographical trends to support data-driven decision-making.

**🎯 Objective**

The primary objective of this project is to:

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Analyze customer purchasing patterns.
* Track fulfillment efficiency across channels.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Discover business opportunities through actionable insights.

Key Features / Highlights
* **📈 Sales Performance Monitoring**-
* Tracked ₹78.59M Total Sales Revenue.
* Analyzed 129K Total Orders across fulfillment channels.
* Monitored 117K units sold with transaction-level insights.
* **🚚 Fulfillment Analysis**-
* Compared Amazon and Non-Amazon fulfillment performance.

* **👥 Customer Segmentation**-

**Key Business Findings**

* ✅ Generated ₹78.59M in total sales revenue.

* ✅ Processed 129K customer orders.

* ✅ Amazon fulfillment accounted for approximately 69.56% of total orders.

* ✅ T-Shirts and Shirts emerged as the highest-selling product categories.

* ✅ Medium and Large sizes showed the highest customer demand.

* ✅ Significant sales concentration observed within a few key product categories.
